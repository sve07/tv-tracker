## Watch list

- [x] Clicking in watch list should open the episode details, not the series overview

- [x] An episode/series should only show up in the watch list after the episode aired

- [x] Order of the series in the list should be sorted top to bottom based on release date (recent first) or the mark as watched date (recent first).

- [x] Series that are tracked but do not have marked episodes need to be in a separate subdivision "Not started yet". By default this section should be hidden, like the 'up-to-date' series section.

- [x] In the series card, the episode to watch next should be displayed e.g. S02E05

## Series overview

- [x] In the series overview, above the episode list, there should be a scrollable timeline of episodes with the "Next episode" to watch in view. This timeline should be scrollable to the left and right to see previous and next episodes. Clicking on an episode in the timeline should open the episode details. The card must also contain the mark as watched button, and the mark as watched button should be replaced with a number in days till air date for episodes that are not yet aired.
